Bulk Excavation

North London Demolition Groundwork regularly carry out bulk excavations on behalf of our clients to make sure that development sites are presented in an optimal condition for redevelopment.

Sites may have been left uneven following previous work, or may require levelling or re-grading to accommodate a change of use at the clients request. Whatever the condition of your site, we have the experience and expertise to help.

By utilising our modern fleet of Excavators, HGV tippers and Dumpers we have established a reputation for a tailored service that suits project timescale criteria.
